## The $1T Problem in Healthcare Triage

Every day, healthcare systems worldwide face a critical challenge: efficiently routing patients to the right level of care. The numbers tell a sobering story:

- **30%** of specialist referrals are inappropriate, costing the US healthcare system alone over $150B annually
- The average patient spends **121 minutes** on healthcare interactions, with only 20% spent with clinicians
- **40%** of patients experience care delays due to misrouting, leading to worse health outcomes

## Current State of Healthcare Triage

### The Broken Status Quo

1. **Manual Triage Bottlenecks**
   - Overwhelmed staff making 100+ routing decisions daily
   - Subjective assessments leading to inconsistent outcomes
   - High burnout rates among triage personnel (45% annual turnover)

2. **Technology Gaps**
   - Legacy systems with limited AI/ML integration
   - Siloed patient data across providers
   - One-size-fits-all decision trees that can't handle complexity

3. **Patient Experience Challenges**
   - Long wait times (average 24 days for new patient appointments)
   - Frustrating, repetitive intake processes
   - Lack of transparency in routing decisions

1. **Technology Readiness**

2. **Regulatory Tailwinds**
   - FDA's AI/ML Software as a Medical Device (SaMD) framework
   - HIPAA-compliant LLM hosting options
   - Growing acceptance of AI in clinical workflows
